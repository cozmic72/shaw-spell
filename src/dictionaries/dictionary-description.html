The Shavian alphabet (also known as the Shaw alphabet) is an alternative alphabet for the English language designed to have a one-to-one correspondence between sounds and letters.

This dictionary, released by joro.io, is part of the Shaw-Dict project, providing comprehensive Shavian-English translation and definition support for macOS.

Compiled using Shavian.info's ReadLex dictionary for translations and IPA transcriptions, and Open English WordNet 2024 for the word descriptions.
