Last Steps: Configure Spell Checking

To enable system-wide spell checking for Shavian in Notes, Pages, Mail, Safari and other apps:

  1. Click "Open System Settings" below, or
    Open system settings → Keyboards → Input Sources
  2. Select Input Sources
  3. In the Spelling dropdown, choose British English (Shaw-Spell) or U.S. English (Shaw-Spell)
  4. Close System Settings
  5. Restart any apps you want to use spell checking in

The spell checker automatically detects whether you're typing in Shavian or English and checks against the appropriate dictionary.

You're all set! Enjoy using Shaw-Spell on your Mac.
