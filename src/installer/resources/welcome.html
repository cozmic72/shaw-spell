Welcome to Shaw-Spell

Shaw-Spell brings comprehensive Shavian language support to your Mac with dictionaries and intelligent spell checking. Built on ReadLex and Open English Wordnet.

  • Three dictionaries for Dictionary.app
    Shavian → English, English → Shavian, and Shavian → Shavian
  • System-wide spell checking
    Works in TextEdit, Pages, Mail, and all native macOS apps
  • Also handles orthodox spelling
    so you can use Shaw-Spell as your default spell checker
  • 75,000+ Shavian words
    With definitions, pronunciations, and usage examples

This installer will install both British and American English variants. You can choose which to use in your system settings.
